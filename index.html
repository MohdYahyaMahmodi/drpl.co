drpl.co

The easiest way to share files between devices on the same network

How it works

drpl.co allows you to share files directly between devices on the same network without any setup or accounts.

Simply open drpl.co on both devices and they will automatically discover each other.

Click on a device to send files or messages securely using peer-to-peer technology.

No data is stored on any server, everything is transferred directly between devices.

Open source and free to use.
