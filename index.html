drpl.co

The easiest cross-platform alternative to AirDrop and SnapDrop

How it works

drpl.co allows you to share files directly between devices on the same network without any setup or accounts - just like AirDrop but for all devices.

Simply open drpl.co on any device - Android, iPhone, Windows, Mac, Linux - and they will automatically discover each other.

Click on a device to send files or messages securely using peer-to-peer technology. No file size limits!

No data is stored on any server, everything is transferred directly between devices with end-to-end privacy.

Why use drpl.co?

Cross-platform: Works on all major devices and operating systems, unlike AirDrop (Apple only).

Privacy-focused: Unlike cloud sharing services, your data goes directly between devices without any servers in between.

No limits: Send files of any size or type without restrictions. Perfect replacement for SnapDrop.

Fast & Easy: No sign-ups, no installs, no configuration - just open the website and start sharing.

Open source and free to use. A modern replacement for SnapDrop and alternative to AirDrop for all devices.
